About Us

During our community visits in Hubli, we identified critical challenges affecting the local population. Unemployment, financial struggles, and lack of skill development are major obstacles for many individuals aspiring for a better future.

The Problem

  • Lack of support for small home-based businesses.
  • Financial illiteracy leading to exploitation.
  • Persistent unemployment due to inadequate skills.
  • Unsafe infrastructure like improper electrical wiring.
  • Limited awareness of women's welfare and education.

Our Solution

During our second community visit, we explored the GMR Foundation, which equips individuals with market-relevant skills. Programs like Pratibha help them secure stable employment and improve their quality of life.
